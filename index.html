FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is TempoSwap?
+
TempoSwap is a decentralized exchange built natively on Tempo Network. It uses the enshrined DEX contract to provide token swapping, limit orders, and yield earning with a TSWAP Points system that rewards early users with future token allocations.
How do I get testnet tokens?
+
Go to the Tempo Network faucet and request free testnet tokens (AUSD, BUSD, TUSD). You get 1 million tokens per request. Gas costs are zero on testnet.
What are TSWAP Points and what can I do with them?
+
TSWAP Points are earned by using TempoSwap. When $TSWAP token launches at TGE, your points will determine your token allocation. 40% of all tokens go to the community.
Why do I need to connect my Twitter?
+
Twitter OAuth verification adds an anti-sybil layer to the referral system. Referral rewards only unlock after you verify your Twitter and complete one real transaction. You also earn +30 bonus points for connecting.
Is TempoSwap safe to use?
+
TempoSwap is non-custodial. All swaps go directly through Tempo Network's enshrined DEX contract. The frontend is open source and verifiable. On testnet, only test tokens are used so there is no real financial risk.
When is mainnet and TGE?
+
We are currently in the testnet community-building phase. Mainnet will follow when Tempo Network mainnet is fully live. TGE will be announced with sufficient notice. Follow @bdggts on X for the latest updates.
